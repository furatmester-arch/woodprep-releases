2. How We Use Your Data

  • To activate and verify your license on each use

  • To improve the software based on usage patterns (aggregated, not personal)

We do not use your data for advertising and do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following trusted service providers, and only to the extent necessary:

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe is PCI DSS compliant. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Registration data (name, email, Machine ID, subscription status) is stored securely on our Azure server.
  • All communication between the plugin and the server is encrypted via HTTPS (TLS).
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, and for up to 12 months after account closure for legal and accounting purposes.
  • Machine IDs are stored as one-way hashes — the original hardware identifiers cannot be reconstructed from stored data.
